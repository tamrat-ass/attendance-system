## ğŸ†˜ **Troubleshooting**

### **Common Issues**

#### **Login Problems**
- **Issue**: Cannot login
- **Solution**: 
  1. Check internet connection
  2. Verify credentials work on web app
  3. Try restarting the app

#### **Sync Issues**
- **Issue**: Data not syncing
- **Solution**:
  1. Check internet connection
  2. Try "Sync Now" in settings
  3. Restart the app

#### **App Crashes**
- **Issue**: App closes unexpectedly
- **Solution**:
  1. Restart the app
  2. Clear app cache in settings
  3. Reinstall if problem persists

#### **Slow Performance**
- **Issue**: App is slow
- **Solution**:
  1. Clear cache in settings
  2. Close other apps
  3. Restart your device

### **Error Messages**

#### **"Network Error"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
- Wait and try again later

#### **"Authentication Failed"**
- Your session may have expired
- Logout and login again
- Check if your account is still active

#### **"Data Load Failed"**
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the screen
- Contact administrator if persistent
